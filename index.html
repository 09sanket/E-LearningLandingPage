Goverment Polytechnic College Balaghat

Government Polytechnic College Balaghat (MP) was established in July 1962 under Open door policy of Central Government. Initially it was run by a society and became a Government Institute in the year 1984. The area of this college is about 10.50 Acres surrounded by a Wainganga river and a dense teak wood forest. Our college is approved by AICTE and running four diploma courses named Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Comp. Sc. & Engineering affiliated to Technical University RGPV, Bhopal Madhya Pradesh.
